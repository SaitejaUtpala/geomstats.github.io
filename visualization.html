Visualization¶

Visualization for Geometric Statistics.

class geomstats.visualization.Arrow3D(point, vector)[source]¶

An arrow in 3d, i.e. a point and a vector.

class geomstats.visualization.Sphere(n_meridians=40, n_circles_latitude=None, points=None)[source]¶

Create the arrays sphere_x, sphere_y, sphere_z of values to plot the wireframe of a sphere. Their shape is (n_meridians, n_circles_latitude).

Spherical Fibonacci point sets yield nearly uniform point distributions on the unit sphere.

class geomstats.visualization.Trihedron(point, vec_1, vec_2, vec_3)[source]¶

A trihedron, i.e. 3 Arrow3Ds at the same point.

draw(ax, **arrow_draw_kwargs)[source]¶

Draw the trihedron by drawing its 3 Arrow3Ds. Arrows are drawn is order using green, red, and blue to show the trihedron’s orientation.

geomstats.visualization.convert_to_trihedron(point, space=None)[source]¶

Transform a rigid pointrmation into a trihedron s.t.: - the trihedron’s base point is the translation of the origin of R^3 by the translation part of point, - the trihedron’s orientation is the rotation of the canonical basis of R^3 by the rotation part of point.

geomstats.visualization.plot(points, ax=None, space=None, point_type='extrinsic', **point_draw_kwargs)[source]¶

Plot points in the 3D Special Euclidean Group, by showing them as trihedrons.
